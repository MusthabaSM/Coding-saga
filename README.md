WebSprint – Freelance Website Builder for Local Businesses

💡 Overview
WebSprint is a freelance web service designed to help local businesses establish a strong online presence quickly and affordably.

👉 Goal: Help small businesses get modern, responsive websites without complexity
👉 Approach: Simple process → Fast delivery → Professional results

🚀 Project Description
Many local businesses still lack an online presence due to high costs, technical complexity, and lack of awareness.
WebSprint solves this by providing:
Fast website development
Simple client onboarding through a registration form
Clean and modern website designs
Easy communication and delivery process
This platform allows business owners to:
Register their business
Share their requirements
Get a custom-built website
The system focuses on speed, simplicity, and accessibility, making it ideal for small and medium businesses.
